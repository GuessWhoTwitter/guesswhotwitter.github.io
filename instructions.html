  1. Register your username and email with a password
  2. Login with your username and password
  3. You will be presented with an anonymized tweet
    1. Click the the Twitter user's picture to make your guess
    2. If the guess is correct, you'll progress to the next tweet
    3. If you guessed wrong, you'll get 2 more attemps before you LOSE
  4. How To Win
    1. Each correct guess is worth 10 points
    2. When you reach 50 points, you'll progress to the next level
    3. Complete 5 levels and you WIN
  5. Multiplayer
    1. If you want to play with friends, you'll be able to invite them via email address as long as they've previously registered
    2. In multiplayer, once you've made a guess, the next player will be given the same tweet and Twitter users
    3. This will repeat for each subsequent user.
    4. When a user has a successfull match, they'll be taken out of the rotation until everyone guesses correctly or ran out of turns and LOST
  6. Future Enhancements
    1. The category of Twitter users will be selecable (Politicians, Movie Starts, Football Players, etc)
    2. Additional levels of progression (this will be confiruable when you start a new game)
